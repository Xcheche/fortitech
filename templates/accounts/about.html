The mission of the Stellar Development Foundation (SDF) is to promote global financial access, literacy, and inclusion. SDF accomplishes this by expanding worldwide access to low-cost financial services through the development and maintenance of technology and partnerships. SDF’s vision is an open and affordable financial system where people of all income levels can access simple-to-use, secure, and low cost finan-cial services. SDF also aims to empower developers with useful technology to create financial products and services for their communities.
